about us

we're cleaning since 2016

Sukura Cleaning Services Ltd is a cleaning company based in Kigali, Rwanda, operating since 2016. We initially began our journey with Kigali Heights and continue to serve them today. Our operations expanded to KBC, where we secured a two-year contract. Additionally, we provide cleaning services for all branches and the headquarters of NCBA Bank. We are also proud to have contracts with ZEP-RE PTA Reinsurance and MEASK Limited.
